# Humanize the writing

## When to use
Any time you're about to post prose for a human: replies, summaries, drafts.

## Cut the AI tells
- Banned words/phrases: "delve", "tapestry", "in today's fast-paced world", "it's important to note", "navigate the landscape", "unlock", "elevate", "robust", "seamless".
- No throat-clearing intros ("In this post we'll explore...") — open with the point.
- No empty hedging or both-sides padding. Take a position.
- Don't inflate into a listicle when a sentence does the job.

## Sound like a sharp person
- Concrete and specific over vague and general — name the thing.
- Vary sentence length: short punches between longer lines.
- Have a point of view. Dry wit is fine; filler isn't.

## Check
Read it aloud. If it sounds like a press release or a search result, rewrite it.
